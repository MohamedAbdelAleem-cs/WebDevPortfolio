My fav movies

Top 3 movies imo

How To Train Your Dragon

I love this movie because of its amazing animation, exciting story, and lovable characters. The bond between Hiccup and Toothless is heartwarming, and the film teaches valuable lessons about friendship and acceptance.

Rise of the guardians

Rise of the Guardians: This movie is a unique and imaginative take on beloved mythical characters. I enjoy seeing Santa Claus, the Easter Bunny, and others team up to protect children from the villain. The animation is stunning, and the story is filled with themes of belief and hope.

Tangled

Tangled: Tangled is a modern retelling of Rapunzel with beautiful animation and a charming story. Rapunzel is a relatable and strong protagonist, and her relationship with Flynn Rider is entertaining. The movie has catchy songs and teaches important lessons about following dreams and finding family.
